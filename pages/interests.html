Just a quick little list of the things that a Loki enjoys.

  • Power. Did you think anything else would be first.
  • Winning. Yes its very similar to the first item but not wholly the same. Who doesn’t like winning.
  • Chaos. I feel like your not really living unless your giving in to your chaotic side.
  • Thor and Frigga. As much as I have battled my brother over the years I have always looked up to him. I miss my mother dearly.
  • Praise. Who doesn’t like being praised every once in a while. And if the people praising you also want to worship you as the God you are, all the better.
  • Magic Tricks. My dear mother taught me the art of magic, so I continue to use it in her name. Thor has his brute strength and I have my cunning and magic.
  • Attention. Yes, I need to be the center of attention. There I’ve said it. Happy now.
  • Multi-Verse Collectibles. Finding out about other versions of myself I have also learned of worlds where I am merely a character in a comic. It is quite interesting to see myself in such manner. These funko pops are lovely.
